There are a few things about Pioneer that make us stand out...

AutoPen

It is perhaps the most obvious, since it's the primary service that we offer. There are a few other companies who do what we do, but none of them do it better. Signing machines have been on the market for a long time, but David Redmon was the first in the industry to offer his customers the option to use a machine to write variable data with a pen. Other competitors were quick to design their own machines, but they have yet to match our quality, strategy, or capacity.

Quality

It is the point of pride for Pioneer. Conventional mail shops put out prodcuts that are "good enough", but we always strive for perfection. Other shops might throw out pieces that jam in their equipment, but we are willing to go the extra mile to complete or rework. You never know when that one piece that jammed was a $10,000 donor.

Strategy

This is an area where we excel. David has spent many years working with clients and testing different strategies to see what works. You might see some strange things that are time-tested strategies for boosting response. Multiple stamps on the reply envelope, a folded reply envelope, and no-frills note cards work well with nonprofit donors. Post-it notes, color envelopes and bright, shiny cards are favorites with commercial clients.

Results

This is why our clients stick around. An AP package that implements our strategies can easily triple the return on investment that clients see from their conventional mail pieces.

Culture

Last, but certainly not least, this is our reason for being. We are a community, and together we are driven by a desire to serve not only our clients, but one another. When an issue comes up, we don't point fingers and lay blame -- we work together to create and implement lasting solutions, and we grow and learn from those experiences.
