David Redmon, Founder and Managing Partner of Pioneer Direct Marketing, has more than 15 years of experience in the direct mail industry. David is credited in the industry for pioneering the research, development and implementation of AutoPen services to fulfill the handwritten needs of direct mail clients and their donor campaigns. Additionally, he was able to expand AutoPen services to the for-profit industry with abounding success with many national manufacturers. Over the years, David and the employees who are a part of his team have strived to bring 100% client satisfaction in every piece of mail that is produced. We look forward to adding people to our team who will continue to embody the qualities reflected in our Mission Statement.
